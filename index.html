about.

I am a junior full-stack developer who just took a leap of faith and moved to San Francisco from London. I was born here in Tiburon but have lived in England my whole life. I'd never have imagined that I would come to love this discipline, but learning to code has been the most rewarding challenge of my life . Two things that have always interested me are understanding how things work and creating things. These two interests were first satisfied in a love for art, then psychology, and now coding; each one adding value to the next. Tackling logic problems using a medium that allows me to build and create things is what I have come to love in learning the languages of modern technology.

What Next? I recently graduated from the Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly, London. This was a 3-month intensive course teaching full-stack programming. Knowing from this experience that I enjoy a fast-paced learning environment, I am eager to join a medium/large team who I can learn from and stretch my abilities. As a developer I am excited by the prospect of using technology to tackle real world problems and better the lives of people around us. I am looking forward to a career in which I can bring my love for understanding and enhancing human behaviour together with a medium that can do so.

Off The Rail

Duration: 1 week

This is a fully RESTful MERN stack e-commerce/social media platform. Similar to the app 'Depop', users are able to buy and sell items, follow other users, and 'save for later' the items they like. More specifically, it is for buying and selling designer items at reduced prices and therefore has a large focus on how much you are saving. xhibit

Duration: 1 week

This was a paired project. Jumee and I created xhibit - a review site for exhibitions. It is a fully RESTful MEAN stack web application. The site displays a collection of art galleries and lists all of the various exhibtions that are currently going on at each. It also features user authentication, a maps API, comment threads, featured exhibitions and a bookmarking component. To keep track of our progress and manage the workload we used trello technology and held daily stand ups. If you'd like to speak to Jumee or view her other work, head over to her portfolio.

Lenzation

Duration: 1 week

Lenzation is a simple replica of Instagram. It's a fully RESTful, single-page web app which I built using EJS as the templating language. The site includes user authentication and allows users to upload their photos, make comments, like photos and follow other users. In addition to the main newsfeed, each user has a profile displaying only their own photos. This was the first time I had used a templating language and I really enjoyed the dynamic nature of it.

JavaScript | EJS | HTML5 | CSS3 | Node.js | MongoDB | Express
